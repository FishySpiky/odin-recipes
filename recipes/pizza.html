Mike's Homemade Pizza

Description

"A great recipe for homemade pizza dough and sauce. The sauce is especially good. Top with whatever you like."

Pizza, an Italian dish with a round in shape made with a base of leavened wheat dough, topped with tomatoes, cheese, and an assortment of various other ingredients. The taste in your mouth is always great, the layers of bread, sauce, cheese, and toppings always impress me.

When I had a margarita cheese pizza with extra cheese on it for the first time, it became my favourite Italian food. It was a medium-sized pizza with six servings each person or three servings per person if they ate in pairs. To the touch, the pizza was hot and spongy.

For all us pizza lovers out there, pizza from restaurants may be expensive but, we can make it ourselves at home. This recipe emulates the greatness of a pizza from a restaurant without breaking the bank. Enjoy!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 (.25 ounce) envelope active dry yeast
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons shortening
    •

    Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 6 fluid ounces water
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
